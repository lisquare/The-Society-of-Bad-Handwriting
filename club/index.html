About us

The Society of Bad Handwriting is a club for people who appreciate the beauty and charm of messy, illegible, and sometimes downright awful handwriting. Through their shared love of handwriting, the club's members create a supportive and creative community that celebrates the quirks and idiosyncrasies of all writing styles, whether they are "good" or "bad."
